About Us

CASUAL is a small but nature-rich campsite that opened in 2001. We provide unique information as an outdoor media and portal to make the outdoors more familiar and accessible.

A while ago, camping was an extraordinary thing. But recently, it has become very familiar with the popularity of camping-themed YouTube videos. It's casual and enjoyable.

Camping for the entire family and a close group is exciting, and also solo camping is a fun way to enjoy. The evolutions around the outdoors, such as easy-going campsites, lightweight and functional tools, and easy-set-up tents, will help you dive into nature.

Glamping is also your option. It's a luxury and glamorous camping style with comfortable amenities, full-size beds, a sofa, a dining set, meals, a coffee maker, staging, etc. Find a style that suits you.
